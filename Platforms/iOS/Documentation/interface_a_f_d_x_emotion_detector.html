Detailed Description

This class utilizes Affdex's emotion capabilities by analyzing video frames and returning the emotions expressed in each frame.

The AFDXEmotionDetector class analyzes successive video frames in order to measure the likelihood of emotional states of the subject. Depending upon the initialization method used, the video frames may come from either the built-in camera of the device, via a file on the device, or pushed to the engine through a method.

usingCamera: (AFDXCameraType)  camera 

Initializes the ADFXEmotionDetector for processing video from a camera.

This method initializes the Affdex emotion engine and prepares it to receive video frames from the camera on the device. The caller specifices which camera (front or back) will be used to capture the frames.

Note that the object "owns" the camera device and will not reqlinuish it until the object is deallocated.

- (void) start

Starts the AFDXEmotionDetector's detection process.

When an AFDXEmotionDetector receives this message, it will begin processing frames from the built-in camera or file (assuming the related initialization methods were used), looking for emotions.

- (void) stop

Stops the AFDXEmotionDetector's detection process.

When an AFDXEmotionDetector receives this message, it will stop processing frames.
